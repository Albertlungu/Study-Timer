🎯 WHAT IT DOES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Automatically tracks your study sessions by monitoring:
  • Applications you use (Obsidian, Notes, Google Docs, etc.)
  • Files you work on (tracks specific documents!)
  • Websites you visit (study sites vs... other sites 😅)
  • Time spent on each activity

No manual timers. No button pressing. Just study (or procrastinate) and it tracks!


📊 FEATURES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  ✅ Automatic tracking - runs silently in background
  ✅ Beautiful web dashboard - real-time stats with charts
  ✅ File-level tracking - knows WHICH documents you work on
  ✅ Browser URL extraction - Chrome, Safari, Firefox supported
  ✅ Study vs. Procrastination - categorizes your activities
  ✅ Session history - see timeline of your day
  ✅ Weekly charts - visualize study patterns
  ✅ Study streaks - track consecutive study days 🔥
  ✅ IB-themed humor - because you need to laugh to survive
  ✅ Privacy first - all data stored locally, never leaves your Mac
